# Acceptable Use Policy

AdBotHost is only for small Python or Node.js Telegram bots.

Disallowed use includes:

- AI model hosting or GPU-style workloads
- crypto mining, mining pools, or mining helpers
- proxies, VPNs, SOCKS5 services, tunnels, or relay abuse
- browser automation, scraping, Selenium, Playwright, Puppeteer, or Chromedriver workloads
- spam, mass DM bots, phishing, malware, credential theft, token theft, or keylogging
- fake ad views, payment fraud, coupon abuse, chargeback abuse, or repeated false payment proofs
- illegal activity or attempts to evade platform limits

AdBotHost may suspend users, reject uploads, stop bots, reject payment requests, disable coupons, and deduct credits for abuse or policy violations.

Admin deductions are recorded in credit history and audit fields. Transaction history must not be deleted.
